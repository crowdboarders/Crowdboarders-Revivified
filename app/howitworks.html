How it works!




You can create one of 6 types of CrowdBoards





If you build a Equity, Lending or Reward CrowdBoard you recieve the funds once 100% of what you are asking for has been raised, there are no time limits whatsoever. When CrowdBoarders invest money their cards are not charged untill the CrowdBoard is funded.




If you build Gift, Donation or Junior CrowdBoard you receive money in real time untill your CrowdBoard reaches 100%. CrowdBoarders cards are charged straight away and money is deposited into you Balanced account you set up when you build your CrowdBoard, this can be linked directly to your Bank Account.






One thing people find suprising is how much we charge for someone to create a CrowdBoard and raise money. We are proud to say we are the only place in the world where you can raise money for anything for FREE!!



So to sum things up: it is simple, effective and FREE! What are you waiting for?
